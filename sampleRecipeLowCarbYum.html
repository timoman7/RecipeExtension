Stuffed Tomatoes with Meat and Cheese

Unsure what to do with large summer garden crop? These cheesy meat stuffed tomatoes are always a hit with family and friends.

Prep Time 15 minutes
Cook Time 8 minutes
Total Time 23 minutes
Servings 10 people
Calories 286 kcal

Ingredients

  • 10 medium tomatoes
  • 1 pound sausage or other ground meat
  • 1/2 cup Monterey Jack cheese
  • 10 slices thin Monterey Jack cheese
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil for greasing
  • Fresh Parsley for garnishing

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F

  2. Peel of skin of sausage. Mince sausage or use a food processor to ground meat.

  3. In a slightly greased skillet, brown sausage for a few minutes.

  4. Properly wash tomatoes with cold water. Wipe with clean cloth.

  5. Make a thin slice at the end of tomatoes, allowing each tomato to stand properly. Slice the top part of tomato to make an opening.

  6. Spoon the center of each tomato, remove seeds.

  7. Slightly brush the outer sides of tomatoes with olive oil.

  8. Add thin slices of cheese around the insides of tomatoes and fill each one with cooked sausage. Top with grated cheese.

  9. Bake for 5 to 8 minutes or until cheese is melted and golden. Feel free to add more cheese if you want cheese to overflow or drip on the sides.

  10. Allow to cool for 1 to 2 minutes and slowly transfer to a serving platter.

  11. Garnish with fresh minced parsley.
